Our story

Dallas's Reliable Auto Repair Team

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe on the road.

Contreras Auto Repair LLC was founded with a simple mission: deliver trustworthy auto repair to the Dallas community. Our founder, a seasoned mechanic with over two decades of experience, saw a need for transparent service where customers are treated like family. We started small, but our commitment to quality and honesty quickly earned us a loyal following.

Today, we continue that tradition. Every repair is backed by our dedication to excellence, and every customer is greeted with respect. We invest in the latest tools and ongoing training to ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible.
