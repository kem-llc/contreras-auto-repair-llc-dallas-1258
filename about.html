Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Auto Repair Shop

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe on the road, with transparency and integrity at every step.

Contreras Auto Repair LLC was founded in 2010 with a vision to offer Dallas drivers a better auto repair experience. Owner Juan Contreras, a master technician with over 15 years of experience, wanted to build a shop where customers felt respected and informed. Starting with a small two-bay garage, the business grew through word-of-mouth thanks to its commitment to quality and fairness.

Today, we serve thousands of satisfied customers from Lakewood to White Rock and beyond. Our team continues the tradition of honest diagnostics, fair pricing, and expert repairs. We treat every vehicle as if it were our own, because your safety and satisfaction are what drive us.
