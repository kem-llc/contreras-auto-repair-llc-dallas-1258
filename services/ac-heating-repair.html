A/C & Heating Repair

Keep your cabin comfortable year-round with expert A/C and heating service.

Schedule this service

Overview

A malfunctioning A/C or heater can make driving miserable. We diagnose and repair all climate control systems, from refrigerant leaks to blower motor issues.

What's included

  • Restored comfort in all seasons
  • Improved air quality and defrosting performance
  • Efficient system operation saves fuel

How it works

1

System Diagnosis

We check refrigerant levels, compressor, condenser, and electrical components.

2

Leak Detection & Repair

We find and fix leaks using UV dye and professional tools.

3

Recharge & Test

We recharge the system to manufacturer specs and verify proper cooling/heating.

Common questions

Why is my A/C blowing warm air?

Common causes include low refrigerant, a bad compressor, or an electrical issue. We can diagnose it.

How long does an A/C repair take?

Most repairs take 2-4 hours, but complex issues may require longer.
